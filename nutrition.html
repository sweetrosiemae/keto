Nutritional Info

Crust Ingredients

Almond flour, organic egg, baking soda, olive oil, mozzarella, psyllium husk, coconut flour (spices) garlic, sea salt, pepper, Italian spices.
Allergy Alert: Contains Nuts & Dairy

*Based on the Margarita Madness pizza

Cooking Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Take packaging off the frozen pizza and place on a pizza pan, or regular pan to fit. Do not put directly on rack. Bake for 18 minutes, if you like it softer, and broil for a few minutes at end for a crispier crust. Take out of oven and let sit for 5 minutes. Cut and enjoy!!!
