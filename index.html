The Pizza

Hello pizza lovers!! I am so happy to bring you the best keto pizza in the WORLD!! Okay maybe not the world....yet! I know first hand how challenging a diet can be, so as I went on my journey I noticed how few options there was at the grocery store or even for delivery in Toronto.

Finally you don't have to sacrifice taste for your diet!

My Story

A little about me...I am a performer and I’ve also had an extensive career in hospitality, from the kitchen to the floor to management. I have always loved cooking, and I wanted to create a delicious pizza for keto and grain/gluten free lifestyles alike. I have been perfecting this recipe for over a year, and I'm beyond excited to launch a keto pizza that is 2g of carbs per slice with all REAL ingredients that you can actually pronounce!

Want to know why I choose this name? Well...whenever people try it they say 'Can’t believe it’s Keto'

I live gluten & grain free, and when I want pizza there are only vegan or cauliflower crusts to choose from. They are not tasty. Then I tried 'Can’t Believe It’s Keto' pizza and it was actually so yummy and satisfying, I’m hooked!!

-Andrea
